Branding

Brand Identity _ Uplift

Uplift is my thesis project at Niagara College. This is a subscription box that helps to alleviate social isolation and improves seniors’ quality of life. Uplift is the brand name for the program. As the definition of the word, this program uplifts seniors and their lives. Considering seniors' ageing vision, it was designed with the strongest colour contrast.

Abobe Photoshop / Abobe Illustrator / Abobe Indesign

Corporate Identity _ NEOBLOCK

The business sector is an educational toy company. The product is a kind of flexible wire covered with silicon, which is an innocuous substance to the human body. Also, there are two neodymium magnets that each wire can connect. The main design concept is inspired by the flexibility of the main feature of the product. In addition, Neoblock is the main winner for the #heyRGDmar Challenge for “branding and identity design.”

Abobe Photoshop / Abobe Illustrator / Abobe Indesign
