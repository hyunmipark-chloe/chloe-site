youthink

This website intends to help teens with drug and alcohol addiction. It also aims to help the youth struggling with mental health, such as anxiety, ADHD, insecurity, and more. The website’s clean and organized layout arouses the teens’ interest and attention through images, colours, and illustrations. Through this layout, the target audience can rely on this website for engagement. In addition, this website would be a hub for teens to connect with various websites and resources on a local and national scale.
