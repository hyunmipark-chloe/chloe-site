Editorial

Thesis _ Thesis Book [Uplift]

The thesis topic is to solve and help social isolation on seniors’ quality of life and well-being with graphic design. Seniors’ social isolation has become a huge problem to deal with. Lately, due to covid-19, they have become the most fragile group in every portion of their lives. So, it is a challenge for graphic design as a profession to create a solution to their problem. You can also take a look the Uplift through Instagram.

Abobe Photoshop / Abobe Illustrator / Abobe Indesign

Thesis _ Uplift : Activity Book / Magazine

The cover of the activity book is designed with the concept of boosting up the seniors’ brains. The interior layout of the activity book is minimized design elements so that people can concentrate on each activity. In terms of the magazine, bright and clean photo images convey a positive and active feeling to seniors. It is designed for seniors to engage with articles by using the image associated with the message of each article. The simple and limited use of typeface increased the readability of the elderly.
